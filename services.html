Our Services

Integrated logistics solutions designed for security, speed, and scalability.

Warehousing & Storage

Secure, state-of-the-art warehouse facilities in North Bengaluru with 24/7 CCTV monitoring, access control, and climate-managed zones. Real-time inventory visibility through integrated WMS.

  • Short and long-term storage contracts
  • SKU-level tracking and batch management
  • Cross-docking and value-added services
  • Scalable space for seasonal peaks

3PL & Fulfillment

End-to-end third-party logistics including receiving, put-away, pick-pack-ship, and returns processing. Seamless integration with your eCommerce platform or ERP.

  • Same-day and next-day dispatch
  • Custom packaging and kitting
  • Returns management and QC
  • Order accuracy above 99%

Last-Mile Delivery

Reliable final-mile distribution across Bengaluru and Karnataka with live tracking, proof-of-delivery, and flexible delivery windows for B2B and B2C shipments.

  • Hyperlocal and regional coverage
  • COD and prepaid handling
  • Delivery attempt notifications
  • Reverse logistics support

Transportation & Distribution

FTL and LTL freight services connecting your warehouse to regional distribution centers, retail stores, and customer locations across South India.

  • Full truckload and part-load options
  • Route optimization and scheduling
  • Temperature-controlled transport available
  • Inter-state freight partnerships

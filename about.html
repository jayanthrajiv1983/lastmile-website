Our Story

Our Story

Lastmile Logi Solutions LLP was founded in 2019 with a clear mission: to provide secure, efficient warehousing and logistics services that help B2B businesses focus on growth rather than supply chain complexity.

Based in North Bengaluru, we started with a single warehouse facility and a commitment to treating every client's inventory with the same care as our own. Today, we offer a full suite of services — from storage and 3PL fulfillment to last-mile delivery and regional transportation.

Our team combines operational expertise with modern technology to deliver transparent, scalable logistics solutions. Whether you're an eCommerce brand scaling across India, or an FMCG distributor managing high-velocity inventory, we adapt to your needs.
